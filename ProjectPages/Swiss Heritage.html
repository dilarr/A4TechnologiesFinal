Project Overview

Swiss Heritage required a modern digital solution to transform their traditional paper-based registration system into an efficient, scalable digital platform.

The goal was to enhance data accuracy, improve client engagement, and enable real-time reporting capabilities for better decision-making.

Issue

The client registration process currently relies on a manual, paper-based system, which has proven to be inefficient and cumbersome. This approach makes it challenging to accurately track client registrations, as well as manage and maintain contact information. As a result,there is a significant risk of lost documents, miscommunication, and delays in processing. The lack of a streamlined system not only hinders organizational efficiency but also affects the quality of service provided to clients. An upgrade to a digital registration system could greatly enhance tracking capabilities and improve overall communication.

A4Technologies Approach

  • Implemented custome CRM platform to enter customer data
  • Embedded the online registration to current company website
  • Store customer information in a SharePoint
  • Generate Power BI report platform
